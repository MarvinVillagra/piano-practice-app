    🎯 Circle of Fifths

    Master the circle - learn scales and chords in order

    The Circle of Fifths shows the relationship between keys. Moving clockwise adds sharps, counter-clockwise adds flats.

    🎓 Learning Strategy:

    • C Major: No sharps/flats - start here!
    • G Major: 1 sharp (F#) - easy transition
    • D Major: 2 sharps (F#, C#) - same fingering
    • Learn one scale per week - master before moving on
    • Practice chord qualities for each key
